Contracts, Agreements and Employees Management. Generate contract from Sale Order whit Product of type "Service".

For this module the user can make the following options:

  • Generate contract from Sale Order with Product od type "Service"
  • Management Contract, Employee
  • Management Agreement. Agreement Type
  • Workflow of contract and sale order
  • Management professional category, Educational level, Jobs

The workflow from a sales order:

  • Sales order in offer status
  • Collective Agreement linked to request
  • Check availability of employees
  • Generate the associated contracts from the sales order lines and the delivered quantity specified in the lines of the same. And depending on the availability of employees.
  • Follow the workflow of contracts

Future developments:

  • Machine Learning Integration
  • Register contracts in INEM, Social Security
  • Reporting System
